What is Neuroloop?

Neuroloop is an extended reality or XR app designed to help you understand how your brain responds to social media. It focuses on improving your time and attention management in both the digital and real worlds. By encouraging reflection on your social media habits, it empowers you to think critically and to use your time and energy wisely. Here's how to get started:

  • Click Sign up and fill out the form

    Once the form is submitted, your profile will be created, and from there you'll be able to easily track your progress.

  • Download Neuroloop from the app store using your XR headset

    Log in to your profile with your username and password.

  • Choose a scenario

    Take a course to learn about your brain, social media use, and how habits form.

Habit theory

How habits form

CUE

The cue is what gets the habit started, it's like a trigger.

Take your phone for example. Think of that notification sound, vibration, or the red badge on your phone that tells you there's a new message. It grabs your attention and makes you want to check your phone.

CRAVING

The craving is what makes you want to do something about the cue.

When you see that notification, you start wondering, "Who messaged me? What's it about?". You feel a sense of anticipation and excitement that makes you want to check your phone.

ROUTINE

The routine is the action you take because of the craving.

You unlock your phone, open the app, and check your messages. Over time, this routine becomes almost automatic and you do it without even thiking.

REWARD

The reward is what makes it all worth it.

Maybe it's a funny meme from a friend, a juicy piece of gossip, or just the satisfaction of not missing anything important. That good feeling is what keeps the habit going. Basically, tech apps use this loop to keep us hooked, and let's be honest, it works!
